Our Story

Founded in April 2024, Bhawani Jewellers is a family-owned micro-enterprise dedicated to creating exceptional jewellery pieces that celebrate life's most precious moments.

Our workshop in Palghar combines traditional Indian jewellery-making techniques with contemporary designs, ensuring each piece tells its own unique story.

Our Mission

To craft jewellery that becomes family heirlooms - pieces that are cherished today and treasured by future generations.

MSME Certified

As a registered micro-enterprise, we take pride in contributing to India's growing jewellery manufacturing sector while maintaining the highest quality standards.
